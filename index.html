About Me

Hello World! Its Prince 'OBEC MUSIC' Olubari. I am a Nigerian singer/songwriter who resides in the UK. I am an educator and lover of good sound and music. My music journey started from RCCG Winners Parish in Rivers State, Nigeria. I have contributed to various church choirs which has given me the opportunity to develop my talent, while worshipping God. I want to say thank you to my supporters and ever increasing fanbase.I appreciate that you have all joined me on my musical journey to the top.
